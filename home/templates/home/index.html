Pride Amsterdam 2022

One of Europe’s most popular gay festivals. Pride Amsterdam 2022 is taking place from 20 July to 7 August – unlike most cities, this one takes place on a canal!

start date: 30-Jul-2022 | finish date: 07-Aug-2022
Lesbian and Gay City Festival 2022

Returning for the 28th time in 2022, The Lesbian and Gay City Festival in Berlin is Europe’s largest street party of its kind. Taking place around Nollendorfplatz, the traditional gay stomping ground in Berlin-Schöneberg, this celebration draws in more than 400,000 visitors each year. This makes the area one of Berlin’s most popular neighborhoods for the weekend.

start date: 16-Jul-2022 | finish date: 17-Jul-2022
Dublin Pride 2022

In 1974 just ten people marched for one of the first pride events in Dublin. Over sixty-thousand people showed up in 2019. Dublin Pride 2022 will be taking place from June 22nd to June 26th, with the main Pride march and parade taking place on Saturday 25 June at 12pm. Throughout the whole month of June, you can expect a wide range of pride events in the city and the county!

start date: 22-Jun-2022 | finish date: 26-Jun-2022
Mighty Hoopla 2023

Mighty Hoopla is the biggest LGBT pop festival in London. It’ll be taking place in Brockwell Park on June 3rd 2023. Headliners are yet to be announced. Previous headliners have included All Saints, Chaka Khan and Bananarama. Headliners for 2022 included the Sugababes, Anastacia and Kim Wilde. It’s guaranteed to be one of the most fun gay events in London next summer. Tickets on sale now.

start date: 03-Jun-2023
Madrid Pride Orgullo MADO 2022

Madrid Orgullo ‘MADO’ is perhaps the largest and one of the most popular gay Pride events in all of Europe, attracting two million people each year. In 2022, this 10-day festival is taking place from 1st to 10th July. Expect fantastic shows, activities, dance parties, club nights, lots of food, drinks and fun surprises at Madrid Pride. It takes place in the Chueca neighborhood where you’ll find Madrid’s extensive gay scene.

start date: 01-Jul-2022 | finish date: 10-Jul-2022
San Francisco Pride 2022

San Francisco Pride holds one of the oldest and largest annual LGBTQ+ parades in the world. With hundreds of thousands of people flooding into the city every year to experience the parade and immerse themselves in the celebrations. The festival includes 20+ live stages at the Civic Centre Plaza, showcasing the diverse talent within the LGBTQ+ community in San Francisco and beyond. San Francisco Pride 2022 is taking place on Sunday, 26 June 2022.

start date: 26-Jun-2022
